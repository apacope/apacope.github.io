    Our Nursery Program

Welcome to the GS APACOPE nursery program.

Nursery Program

Our nursery program gives three and four-year-olds a head-start to an education in a fun environment! Students are taught phonics, numbers, colors, and shapes with a fun theme each week.

Social skills, and motor skills, along with French and English are also introduced in this program.

Our teachers enable children to achieve their full personal potential in our bright learning facility.

Students completing nursery at GS APACOPE enter Primary with the skills they need for a successful experience.
